Fitness & Amenities

Stay active during your stay with our fully equipped gym and additional facilities designed for your comfort and convenience.

Fitness Center

Our state-of-the-art fitness center features modern cardio and strength training equipment to help you maintain your workout routine while traveling.

Open daily: 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Treadmills and elliptical machines
  • Free weights and weight machines
  • Yoga mats and exercise balls
  • Towel service
  • Water station

Business Center

Stay productive with our fully equipped business center, offering everything you need for work or meetings.

Open daily: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • High-speed internet
  • Printing and scanning services
  • Private meeting rooms
  • Video conferencing equipment
  • Administrative assistance (on request)

Swimming Pool

Relax and unwind at our outdoor swimming pool, surrounded by comfortable loungers and tropical landscaping.

Open daily: 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Heated pool
  • Poolside service
  • Towel service
  • Lounge chairs and umbrellas
  • Children's pool area

Parking

Convenient and secure parking options for all our guests.

Available 24/7
  • Complimentary self-parking

  • Direct access to hotel
