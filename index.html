Discover Epe Town

Epe Town, the vibrant heart of Epe LGA in Lagos State, is a hidden gem blending cultural heritage, natural beauty, and bustling local life. Here are some highlights of six key areas to explore:

Oluwo Fish Market Area

Experience Epe’s fishing culture at the lively Oluwo Fish Market—fresh seafood, local delicacies, and the colorful Kayokayo Festival await you.

Oba’s Palace Area (Oke-Balogun)

Visit the Oba’s Palace for a taste of Yoruba tradition, historic architecture, and vibrant festivals in Epe’s cultural center.

Epe Marina Waterfront

Relax by the scenic lagoon, enjoy boat rides, and savor local snacks at this tranquil waterfront—especially lively during the Kayokayo Festival.

Epe General Hospital Area

See the community’s daily life, sample street food, and experience the warmth of Epe’s people around this local hub.

Epe Divisional Library Area

Enjoy a peaceful break at the library, explore local history, and connect with students in this calm, educational spot.

Isale Eko Area (Epe Main Market)

Dive into the bustling Epe Main Market—shop for fresh produce, clothes, and souvenirs, and soak in the lively Saturday market scene.
