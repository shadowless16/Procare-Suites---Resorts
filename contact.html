Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 1:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included with the room?

Breakfast is included with Deluxe, Executive, and Presidential suites. For other room types, breakfast can be added for an additional fee.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all guests staying at Procare Suites & Resorts.

How can I make a restaurant reservation?

You can make a restaurant reservation by contacting us directly via phone, email, or through our contact form on this page.
